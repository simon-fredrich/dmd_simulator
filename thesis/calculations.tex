\section{Dipta}
The position of a point source is 
\begin{equation*}
    \mathbf{p} = \left(\begin{matrix} x\\y
    \end{matrix}\right)
\end{equation*}
whilst the rotated position around the angle $\theta$ would be
\begin{equation*}
    \mathbf{p'}=\left(\begin{matrix}
        \cos(\theta) & -\sin(\theta)\\ 
        \sin(\theta) & \cos(\theta)
    \end{matrix}\right) \cdot \mathbf{p}
\end{equation*}
